FAQ
App stuck on splash screen?▼
ION v1.0 loads even without internet. If you see a long splash, it may be the first load fetching fonts. After first install via PWA, it works 100% offline. Make sure you're not blocking the app in mobile settings.
Why isn’t GPS working?▼
Tap the GPS pill in the topbar to re-prompt. iOS: Settings → Safari → Location → Allow. Android: Chrome app permissions → Location. Tap the compass ring to request orientation permission on iOS.
How do I track a flight or train?▼
Go to the Flight Deck tab (airplane icon). Type a flight number like KL719, a train number like IC3402, or a bus line like Bus 1. Tap Track. ION fetches live status, route info and saves it for continuous updates.
How does offline map download work?▼
On the Maps tab, tap 📦. A resizable selection box appears. Drag it to your area and resize with the corner handle. Tap "Download Sector" — a real progress bar shows tile caching. Tiles stay available even offline.
Can I use ION for any city’s weather?▼
Yes. Type any city in the Weather tab search bar and tap GO. Your live GPS stays active for telemetry.
